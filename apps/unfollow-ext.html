Features

Take control of your social feed

🕵️

Follower Audits

Runs directly inside the browser using your local session to safely detect non-followers without logging in to external services.

⚡

Bulk Actions

Provides a simple interface to view unfollow lists, queue actions, and clean up your feed efficiently.

🛡️

Local & Private

No passwords or personal data are ever uploaded. Zero-knowledge and local-first execution.
