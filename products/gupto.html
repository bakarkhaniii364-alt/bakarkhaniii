Features

Privacy that doesn't ask for trust

End-to-End Encrypted

Your data is encrypted before it leaves your device. Not even we can read it.

📵

Local-First

Works offline. Everything stored locally unless you explicitly choose to sync.

Rich Notes

Choose to sync across devices with your own key. Zero-knowledge by design.
